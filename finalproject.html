Final Project

So for the Final Project of the CSC 106 course, me and my partner making a game called "BlackJack!"
To make this game, me and my partner working together to make the Blackjack game look and works like the real game itself.
The player can choose poker chips and betting to amount of money that the player want,
the card that they receive is also random for each game, and we try to make the game appearance
looks exactly like a real blackjack table.

To help the player play the game, we also add an instruction button
as a guidance on how to play the game for people who don't understand how the game works.
The instructions:
* To begin, each player is dealt two cards (from a normal deck of 52).
* All cards have value of 2-10. (Face cards = 10). An Ace can be 1 or 11.
* The goal is for the total of the cards to be as close to 21 as possible.
* If the total of the cards is exactly 21 you win.
* If the total of the cards goes over 21 you lose.
* If there is a tie, the hand will continue until there is a winner.
* Betting increments are set with: 5$, 10$, 15$, and 20$ chips.
